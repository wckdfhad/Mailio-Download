# Mailio

**Mailio** is a desktop email client for Windows that lets you manage multiple email accounts from a single app.

## What Mailio Does

Mailio connects to your email accounts and displays them in one unified inbox. You can:

- **Read, compose, and reply** to emails across all your accounts in one place
- **Search** your inbox instantly or perform deep searches across your full email history
- **Organize** emails with Gmail labels — create, rename, and delete labels directly from the sidebar
- **Star, archive, and snooze** messages to keep your inbox focused
- **Download attachments** from any message

Mailio supports **Google Gmail** (via OAuth) and **IMAP/SMTP** email providers.

## How Mailio Uses Your Data

Mailio requests access to your Google account to provide core email functionality:

- **Read your emails** — to display your inbox, sent mail, drafts, and other folders within the app
- **Send emails on your behalf** — to compose and send new messages or replies from your Gmail address
- **Manage labels** — to let you create, rename, and delete Gmail labels for organizing your mail
- **View your profile information** (name and email address) — to identify your account within the app

Mailio does **not**:

- Sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties
- Use your data for advertising or analytics
- Store your emails on external servers — all data stays between your device and Google's servers
- Access your account when you're not actively using the app
